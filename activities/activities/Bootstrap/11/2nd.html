The Red Studio features a small retrospective of Matisse's recent painting, sculpture, and ceramics,
displayed in his studio. The artworks appear in color and in detail, while the room's architecture and
furnishings are indicated only by negative gaps in the red surface. The composition's central axis is
a grandfather clock without hands - it is as if, in the oasis of the artist's studio, time were suspended.
