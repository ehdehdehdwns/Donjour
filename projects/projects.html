HTML Project: Dreaming Places



Project #1 consists in creating an informative website with HTML.
The information of this website is about Iceland, which is Nordic island country in the North Atlantic, with a population of 360,390 and an area of 103,000 km2 (40,000 sq mi), making it the most sparsely populated country in Europe. There are many beautiful places covered with snow and magnificent Aurora in that place. That's why I choosed this place in my website.

I included few events that travelers could enjoy and approximate price list to visit. There are also contact information before you could comfirm about your travel. There are three more pages at the bottom to browse two different places called The Norther Lights, Hiking of Glaciers, and the last one directs to contact e-mail.
